# ADR-010 — Honest Market Expansion

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** Phase 1 founding
**Owner:** Product + Lead Architect

## Context

"Never fabricate" combined with a national-looking UI in empty markets produces dishonest emptiness — the exact pattern that erodes trust and that we exist to reject.

## Decision

Phase 1 launches in the **Jersey Shore market only**: Stafford, Manahawkin, Barnegat, Long Beach Island, Toms River, Forked River, Waretown, and Tinton Falls, within Ocean and Monmouth Counties. The architecture scales nationally, but the product never pretends to be national before it is. Growth feels earned.

## Alternatives Considered

- **National launch** leveraging Google's national coverage.

## Why Rejected

- A national surface over empty markets looks national but feels empty and fake, violating honest-emptiness and the trust brand.

## Consequences

- Information architecture, SEO, and routing are market-scoped with a clean path to add markets.
- Out-of-area users get an honest "not here yet" experience while Rocco can still help with general guidance and education.
- The service-by-market page matrix is bounded to combinations we can populate honestly — no auto-generated thin national pages.

## Future Impact

Expansion is a deliberate, signaled motion, market by market. The same honest-emptiness discipline governs every future market and every future EMG product.
